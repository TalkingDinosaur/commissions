    About Me

    Hello, I'm Mr.TalkingDinosaur aka Mr. TD! Welcome to my commissions gallery!

    I started commissioning around the start of 2020 when the pandemic began. I started by ordering art of Peri and eventually found Larcei and Seliph to be my eternal muse. I commission other characters and franchises too, but Larcei and Seliph are very near and dear to my heart~

    Since my debut in 2020, and at the time of writing this; I've ordered 500+ individual commissions and produced 3 doujins. I'm currently producing some animations with the ultimate goal to produce my very own OVA; I hope to do so in a few years!

    Thanks for reading this far! Please enjoy the fruits of my creative musings, I hope it may inspire some of you the way others inspired me in my youth from their own creative endeavors!

    And remember: "It doesn't stop." :^)
